Shane Menu

For your starter I have whipped up a delicious
Branding project made with the finest ingredients.
My chef de partie and I created an elegent Irish
Whiskey brand named after Irish Goddess, Eriu.

Time | 4 Weeks

Client | Bord Bia

My Rating | 8/10

process for this project.
Ingredients |

Typeface Design
Image Making
Product Design
Motion Graphics

Idea Generation |

Jim Fitzpatrick | ‘Eriu, Goddess of the Tuatha Dé Danann’, 1976.
During the concept phase we found the story of eponymous
Goddess of Ireland, Eriu. She was known for being a modern
personification of Ireland, who stood for energy and renewal.

Secondary Research |

Explicit Studio | ‘Beaujolais Nouveau’, 2017.
Alex Slobzheninov | ‘Agrandir’, 2018.
Jim Fitzpatrick | ‘Chinatown’, 1976.
We wanted to create a modern visual language for
the brand that succesfully represented Eriu's energetic
character and attracted a new, younger audience.

Primary Response |

By identifying commanlities in old Celtic Type
we were able to manipulate the forms and begin
to create our very own sans serif, ‘Eriu Sans’.
Final Outcome |

Packaging Design |

Once we had designed our very own sans serif
typeface ‘Eriu Sans’, we began to produce designs
for the finalised bottle and packaing for the brand.

Poster Design |

We designed a set of posters in augmented reality
that referenced various social movements in Ireland
in order to attract a younger, more diverse audience.

Shall we move onto Mains?
