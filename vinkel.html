Final Outcome |

Typographic Layouts |

Vinkel reminded my of the typography that appears
on football jerseys so I decided to base the specimen
around football, specifically Danish footballer Jari Litmanen.

Cover & Poster Design |

I developed specific scores for each letter, numeral and glyph
in the typeface in order to create a top trumps type game that
I called 'Type Trumps'. I applied these to the cover and poster.
